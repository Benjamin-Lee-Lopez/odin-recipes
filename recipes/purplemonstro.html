Purple Monstrocity Fruit Smoothie

Description

A blueberry & banana smoothie with a citrus kick.

Ingredients

  • 2 frozen bananas, skin removed and cut into chunks
  • 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey (Optional)
  • 1 teapoon vanilla extract (Optional)

Directions

  1. Step 1
    Place bananas, blueberries and juice in a blender, puree. Use honey and/or vanilla to taste. Use more or less liquid depending on the thickness you want for your smoothie.
